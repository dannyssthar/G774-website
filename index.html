STEP 2

Conduct a Needs Assessment

Book an appointment to analyze your current situation and future goals. This systematic process identifies gaps, strengths, and opportunities, creating a clear roadmap for sustainable development. Advanced tools used include: Questionnaires, Focus Group Discussions (FGD), Key Informant Interviews, GIS, drones, Google Earth, and mapping tools for precision planning, Participatory Learning Action (PLA) and Participatory Rapid Appraisal (PRA) tools

STEP 3

Proposal Development and Design

Collaborate on proposals and project designs using an inclusive approach that captures diverse perspectives and ensures impactful outcomes

STEP 4

Matchmaking with Partners and Funds

Platform Visibility: Proposals are uploaded to the Grassroots 774 platform to provide visibility to potential partners, off-takers, and financiers. AI Matchmaking: The platform uses intelligent AI tools to match your projects with the most suitable funding sources, partners, and off-takers.

STEP 5

Implementation, Monitoring, and Review

Approved projects are implemented with continuous monitoring to ensure success. Grassroots 774 supports you through every stage to maintain alignment with objectives.

STEP 6

Completion and Impact Assessment

Projects undergo final evaluation to measure their impact and ensure sustainable outcomes. Insights from completed projects are used to improve future initiatives.
