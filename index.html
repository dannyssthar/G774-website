ABOUT GRASSROOTS 774

Empowering Communities,

Transforming Futures

Empowering Communities,

Transforming Futures

Grassroots 774 (G774) Limited is a private development agency committed to transforming Nigeria’s 774 Local Government Areas (LGAs) into thriving hubs of socioeconomic innovation and sustainable growth. G774 empowers individuals, businesses, community-based organizations (CBOs), and local authorities by facilitating access to development assistance. This is achieved through fostering innovation, building capacity, and leveraging strategic partnerships.

Guided by the Renewed Hope Agenda of the Federal Government, the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) by 2030, and the African Union’s Agenda 2063, G774 accelerates progress toward an equitable, inclusive, and prosperous Nigeria.
